X.X.X Great Basin Unified Air Pollution Control Division (GBUAPCD)

This data set contains surface meteorological data from the Great Basin Unified Air Pollution Control Division (GBUAPCD). Information on the instruments, data collection and processing are unavailable. There are 6 stations included in this T-REX 2006 Five Minute Surface Composite.

More information on GBUAPCD can be found at http://www.gbuapcd.org/index.htm.
