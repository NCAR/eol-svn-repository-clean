X.X.X NAWS China Lake HANDAR Weather Statin Data [NAWS China Lake]

This data set contains surface meteorological data from teh Naval Air Weapons Station (NAWS) China Lake network of HANDAR weather stations. There are 12 stations included in this T-REX 2006 Five Minute Surface Composite.

The Calculated Sea Level Pressure was computed from station pressure, temperature, dew point, and station elevation using the formula of Wallace and Hobbs (1977).

The dew point was computed from the temperature and relative humidity using the formula from Bolton (1980).
