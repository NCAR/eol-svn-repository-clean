X.X.X Arizona State University (ASU) Environmental Fluid Dynamics Group Flux Tower Sonics

This data set contains surface meteorological data from the Arizona State University (ASU) Environmental Fluid Dynamics Group Sonics Flux Tower placed in Independence, CA for the T-REX project. There is one ASU sonics station included in this T-REX 2006 Five Minute Surface Composite.

The ASU Sonics Flux Tower collected data every 0.1 second. The five minute record for the T-REX 5-minute surface composite is an average of a minimum of 200 non-missing 0.1 second records that were recorded during the prior minute to the five minute record.

Instrumentation

Measurements from the following instrumentation are included in this T-REX 5-minute surface composite.

  • R.M. Young 3D ultra-sonic anemometer at 11.4m AGL
  • Finewire thermocouple at 1.9m AGL (coupled with CS Sonic)
  • Relative Humidity sensor at 9.8m AGL
  • Barometric Pressure gauge at 1m AGL
